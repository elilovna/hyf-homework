Master class from Svetlana Karpova "Photo session of children"

Svetlana Karpova - the leading MC, a professional new born photographer. On this basic master class you will receive the fundamentals of the theory and practice of shooting newborns. Now this is a very demanded, albeit difficult, direction of commercial photography. Participate in a photo shoot of a baby up to 14 days old and you will learn:


  • You will get acquainted with the game and the greatness of the natural light from the window
  • Start creating wonderful frames with a minimum set of details
  • Record the steps of creating priceless photos "baby in the hands of parents, along with older children in the family"
  • We will consider shooting conditions not only in the studio, but also in the home environment, which is not always convenient for the photographer
  • Learn to negotiate with the client, mark important aspects before filming.

Program

10:00 Part I. Introduction. Theory of shooting infants.

  • My personal experience. Where to begin
  • Features in the direction of "Photography of newborns."
  • he shoot place. Preparation of the workspace, the guarantee of successful shots. Shooting under the conditions of natural light. Those cases when we use artificial light, rules, arrangement.
  • Props. Which is better to buy fabrics for shooting, where to order accessories or what you can do yourself. Harmonious combination of colors and shades.
  • Photographing a newborn. The main mistakes and difficulties that arise at the beginning of the activity, how to avoid them. Elena shoots in manual mode, so the basic knowledge of the technical part of your camera is necessary to participate in the master class - shutter speed, aperture and exposure. And also the jpg settings and basic histogram concepts.

12:00 II part. Practice.

  • The length of the survey and the number of images for a single photo session of a children. Simple and complex posing. The technique of winding windings. Creating a mono image. The general plan, a portrait, correct foreshortenings.
  • What you should pay attention to during the composition of the frame, in order to facilitate the further processingprocess.
